## Prototype

[View the Prototype] [The Prototype](https://www.figma.com/proto/oYoJrvAMTyJJKNORnmv51s/BudgetLife-Wireframes?node-id=73-512&p=f&t=rbnFUwsVXjzNpJNp-1&scaling=scale-down&content-scaling=fixed&page-id=0%3A1&starting-point-node-id=73%3A512&show-proto-sidebar=1)

- Main dashboard
- Budget management interactions
- Social features (friends & city visits)
- Profile & account settings
